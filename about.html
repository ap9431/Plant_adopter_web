Helping farmers, saving trees


Nature has always been an intergal part of human life. Without trees and plants, no one can survive. People are understanding the importance of saving and growing trees, but today’s life also brings new challenges to human beings like the challenge of managing one’s time. This challenge has forced many people to leave their thoughts of adopting and looking after plants.


At Plant Adopters, we work as a mediator for a farmer and an adopter to connect together to bring about a change. Farmers need investment to continue with their care for nature and adopters need someone who can take care of their plants. With this, the farmers can use their knowledge on plants and adopters can benefit from the help of the farmers.

Our Values


VISION:
1. Here, at Plant Adopters, we believe in working as a unit with farmers and keeping them in touch with the outside world ready to walk in nature.
2. Also, we be with our adopters by following the policy of transparency, by which the farmer and his associated plant adopter can connect to each other but also we keep in mind the security of the credentials of the farmer or the adopter.
3. Here, at Plant Adopters, we respect the privacy of out adopter friends and our farmer friends.
