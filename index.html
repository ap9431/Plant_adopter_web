Adopting a plant is a new blessing!

Adopt a plant today to help mother nature revive and the care-takers of the nature-our farmers.

Adopting a plant is equivalent to saving out planet, which has become the need of the hour. You may think that it would be difficult for you to look after the plant. But, at Plant Adopters, we are there to help you to reach out to a farmer who would help you to grow trees. Find that adopting a tree is not at all difficult by clicking the Explore button, and start your journey on the flourishment of nature.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I find the plants suited for my locality?

A. Our website has the perfect manner to look up for a plant or a tree by just clicking on the ‘Explore’ button and filtering your search.

2. What all documents are needed to sign up as an adopter/farmer?

A. A passport-sized photograph and an ID ( essentially Aadhar card ) is required to sign up as an plant adopter/farmer.

3. How can I view the details of the farmer or the adopter associated with me?

A. When you log in into your dashboard, you will all the details of your associate farmer or adopter.

4. How can I track the growth of the plant that I adopted?

A. The growth of your adopted plant can be tracked with the help of the ‘Plant Tracker’ tab present in your dashboard.

5. How can I contact the farmer taking care of my adopted plants?

A. Plant Adopters follows the policy of being transparent. So once you adopt a plant, in your dashboard you will find the contact details of the farmer assigned with the task of taking care of your plant.
